Welcome to Our Museum

The La Habra Historical Museum serves as a center for gathering, preserving, and interpreting significant historical artifacts to educate the public about La Habra's rich heritage.

Housed in a historic 1935 WPA (Works Progress Administration) building that originally served as the La Habra library until 1967, our museum showcases over 2,000 artifacts spanning from the mission days through the agricultural era, oil discovery, and the development of our beloved city.
