**Project:** LaHabra Old Settlers Museum Digital Experience
**Phase:** 2 - Ideation

**Client Direction:**
- More modern approach than the current tree visualization
- Use QR codes so visitors use their own devices
- Visitors use earpieces for audio
- Leverage all 24 existing videos

**Immediate Goal:** Set up ONE video as a test anywhere in the museum to let the client experience the concept before committing to full build.

**Why:** Client needs to tangibly experience what QR + personal device + earpiece feels like in the museum context before approving full direction.

**How to apply:** When suggesting implementations, prioritize:
1. Quick, testable prototypes (single video first)
2. QR-code-native flows (no app download, instant mobile web)
3. Audio-first UX (earpiece-compatible, not speaker-dependent)
4. Museum context (variable lighting, older users, gloves possible)
